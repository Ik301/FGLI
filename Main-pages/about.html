About

What do we do, who am I, why did I start this?

Mission

We provide resources to empower First Generation Low Income (FGLI) students, making college admissions more accessible through information and support.

About Me

As a successful FGLI immigrant student, I want to give back by sharing my experience navigating challenges and accessing resources.

Why

I believe FGLI status can be an advantage, but resources are often hard to find. I started this to help FGLI students achieve success through support, as I did.
